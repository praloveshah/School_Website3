Campus Life

School Entrance
Founded in 1959

The Academy at Lincoln is the 2nd best Middle School for VS children in North Carolina. With many extracurricular activities and clubs, it is regarded as one of the most versatile schools for children in North Carolina.



School Lego Robotics Club
Made in 2017

The Academy at Lincoln has many extracurricular clubs and activites. The Lego Robotics Club is one of them. In Lego Robotics, students program and build robots to make them complete a set of tasks. Lincoln Academy in particular seems to be very good at this, making 3 different teams into state during the state competition.



School Band
Made in 2018

While the Band Director, Mr. Parnell, has only been teaching for a few years, do not be fooled. This is one of the best bands in the state. They have multiple "Superior," awards, which is the highest rank achievable in band.

State Lego Robotics
Came 3rd in State Lego Robotics

The Academy at Lincoln came in 3rd place with its State Lego Robotics team, the Robo Quackrs. They got a score of 330, better than some National Level Teams.
